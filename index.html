About ComputeChain

ComputeChain is an experimental Layer-1 blockchain built around a new consensus and incentive model called Proof-of-Compute (PoC) ‚Äî focused on executing useful GPU computations (targeting RTX 4090/5090 and later).

Unlike traditional Proof-of-Work blockchains that waste energy on meaningless hash calculations, ComputeChain enables GPU workers to earn native CPC tokens by performing verifiable, useful computations ‚Äî from AI model training to 3D rendering.

The network currently operates in Stage 4: Proof-of-Compute Framework, featuring stable multi-validator PoA consensus, post-quantum-ready signing architecture, and a gas-based economic model.
